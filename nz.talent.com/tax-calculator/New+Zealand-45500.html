Summary
If you make $45,500 a year living in New Zealand, you will be taxed $7,640. That means that your net pay will be $37,860 per year, or $3,155 per month. Your average tax rate is 16.8% and your marginal tax rate is 23.9%. This marginal tax rate means that your immediate additional income will be taxed at this rate. For instance, an increase of $100 in your salary will be taxed $23.89, hence, your net pay will only increase by $76.11.
Bonus Example
A $1,000 bonus will generate an extra $761 of net incomes. A $5,000 bonus will generate an extra $3,806 of net incomes.
